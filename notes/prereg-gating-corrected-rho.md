## Claim under test (scoped)

For **oracle-located, criterion-qualified absorbed pairs** with leaky encoder
gating, the dominance-partition estimator ρ̂_D (and, secondarily, the
exclusive-ratio estimator ρ̂_X) removes the leak-inflation bias of counting
estimators without any new harness-tuned constant, in both the clean-gating
(σ = 0) and leaky (σ > 0, real-activation) regimes.

Round 9 does **not** test: detection (the frozen detector is not run),
detector FPs/FNs or rate-window selection, automatic orientation as a
validated stage, or end-to-end label-free operation. Oracle information is
used to (i) locate the pair, (ii) orient it, (iii) score — never inside the
estimators.

## Estimators (frozen definitions)

Binarize both latents at θ = 0.05 (the detector's θ). Pattern cells n₁₁,
n₁₀, n₀₁ over the scored token set; on 11-tokens compare raw activations
(valid under the S1 unit-decoder-norm precondition, asserted in code at eval
time on the scored pair's columns, tolerance 1e-3).

- **ρ̂_C** (oracle-comp count; leak-bias baseline) = P(b_comp)/P(b_par ∨ b_comp).
- **ρ̂_X** = n₀₁ / (n₀₁ + n₁₀); undefined if n₀₁ + n₁₀ = 0.
- **ρ̂_D** = n̂_J/(n̂_J + n̂_S); 01 → J, 10 → S, 11 → J iff
  act_comp > act_par (ties → S — arbitrary rule, tie rate reported);
  undefined if n̂_J + n̂_S = 0.
- **ρ̂_count** (incumbent, descriptive): lo = latent with lower binarized
  rate over the full eval set (exact rate tie → comp); ρ̂_count =
  P(b_lo)/P(b_lo ∨ b_hi). Registered scope note: under clean gating this
  targets min(ρ, 1−ρ), so it is a ρ-estimator only for ρ ≤ 0.5; it is NOT
  the leak-bias comparator (that is ρ̂_C).

**Two scoring endpoints per estimator:**

- **M (mechanism):** computed on oracle parent-event tokens only (J ∪ S;
  background excluded by oracle mask). Tests the correction itself.
- **O (operational):** computed on all tokens. Includes background
  contamination; the deployable quantity.

Descriptive readouts (registered, no bars): θ-sensitivity (ρ̂_D-O at
θ ∈ {0.02, 0.10}); auto-orientation (rarity rule; also reported as the
unordered pair {ρ̂_D, 1−ρ̂_D}); signed bias, MAE, RMSE and every seed-level
estimate in the CSV; per-run diagnostics a₁, a₀, g₀, g₁, δ_J, δ_S, π_B,
q₁₁-B, b_p, b_c, B∧11 mass, h_B, tie rate, realized ρ.

## Confirmatory cells (fresh seeds, 24/cell, weights saved)

Synthetic harness (round-8 E3 family: d = 64, 30 orthonormal Bernoulli(0.08)
distractors coeff U(0.8,1.2), m = 32, P_HOST = 0.25, λ = 0.2, amp 1,
15k steps; feature QR seed 100+s, eval generator 9500+s):

- SC: ρ ∈ {0.1, 0.3, 0.5, 0.7} × σ ∈ {0, 0.05, 0.1} × seeds 40–63
  = 288 runs, one batched training.

Real-activation harness (round-8 E1 protocol: GPT-2 layer 6 activation bank
`activations_l6.pt` regenerated by the committed extractor, shared across
train/eval — disclosed; injected orthonormal pair (QR, generator 300+s),
AMP = 5, λ = 1.0, ε = 0.002, m = 128, 20k steps, batch 2048; eval = 200k
fresh draws, generator 9500+s, independent of the training stream
(generator 1); SAE init torch.manual_seed(51) per cell batch — matched
across cells):

- RC: ρ ∈ {0.1, 0.3, 0.5, 0.7} (Q = 0.04/0.36, 0.12/0.28, 0.20/0.20,
  0.28/0.12; Q + P0 = 0.4) × seeds 32–55 = 96 runs, one batched training
  per cell.

Per-seed variation: pair directions (RC) / feature basis (SC), token
sampling, injection stream, noise; SAE init is matched within a batch by
the shared manual_seed (disclosed). Nominal ρ is the scoring target;
realized ρ per eval set is recorded and its deviation reported.

**Qualification (frozen, oracle):** par = argmax_j |cos(D_j, v_p)|,
comp = argmax_j |cos(D_j, w_true)|, w_true ∝ v_p + v_c normalized
(SC: (v_p+v_c)/‖·‖; RC: (a_p+a_c)/√2). Absorbed iff cos_comp > 0.98 AND
par ≠ comp (both harnesses; RC criterion = E1's 0.98). Exact argmax ties:
lowest index (float ties are not expected; rule stated for completeness).
Failed/divergent training runs: excluded, disclosed, never rerun. **No run
may be excluded on the basis of F1–F4 diagnostics, estimator output, or any
quantity downstream of qualification.** Per-cell exclusion (non-formation)
rates are a registered readout; the ρ = 0.1 RC cell (Q = 0.04) is the
a-priori formation risk. **Minimum-inclusion rule:** a cell is scoreable
iff ≥ 16 of 24 seeds qualify; otherwise the cell is reported unscoreable
(this cannot be converted into a pass).

## Registered predictions

Per scoreable cell over included seeds; 10k-resample seed bootstrap CIs
reported alongside (they quantify, not enlarge, the n = 24 information).
Multiplicity stance (acknowledged): every-cell pass with any-cell falsify is
deliberately severe; one aberrant cell decides falsification, and the
per-cell verdicts are the primary record.

- **P1M / P2M (mechanism, primary):** ρ̂_D-M MAE ≤ [AT-LOCK: target 0.03]
  in every RC cell (P1M) and every SC cell (P2M). Falsified if any cell
  > [AT-LOCK: target 0.07]. Bars must be materially tighter than the
  operational bars — the background-free mechanism admits only the δ-term
  budget.
- **P1O / P2O (operational):** ρ̂_D-O MAE ≤ 0.05 in every RC (P1O) / SC
  (P2O) cell; falsified if any cell > 0.15. [AT-LOCK: confirm or tighten
  from the measured h_B/background budget.]
- **P3 (beats the leak-bias baseline):** eligible cells are predefined at
  lock as those where D1's measured leak rates predict ρ̂_C bias ≥ 0.10
  under the §4 formula [AT-LOCK: list the cells]. In every eligible cell,
  MAE(ρ̂_D-O) ≤ MAE(ρ̂_C-O) − 0.05. Falsified by any eligible-cell
  counterexample.
- **P4 (mechanism check, class-separated):** compute δ_J and δ_S per seed
  (each defined only when its class-11 denominator ≥ 100 tokens; smaller →
  that seed contributes no δ estimate, counted and disclosed). Registered:
  cell-median δ_J ≤ 0.05 AND cell-median δ_S ≤ 0.05 in every scoreable
  cell; falsified if either median > 0.10 in any cell. The weighted
  misclassification mass (1−ρ)a₀δ_S + ρg₁δ_J is reported per cell as the
  bias-relevant quantity.
- **P5 (fallback, descriptive):** in cells where |a₀ − g₁| ≤ 0.10 AND the
  measured background-exclusive contamination is approximately symmetric
  (|q₀₁ − q₁₀| ≤ 0.2·(q₀₁ + q₁₀)), ρ̂_X-O bias magnitude ≤ 0.10; also
  reported: the §4 odds-formula *prediction* of ρ̂_X from measured
  (a₀, g₁, q₀₁, q₁₀) vs its observed value. No pass/fail bar.

Honest-scoring rule: all endpoints scored as registered, failures included,
in `results/round9/SUMMARY.md`; report/PAPER text follows the outcome.
